How Project Submission Works

Our project submission process ensures that your request receives the support it needs while maintaining transparency and trust for our donors.

  1. Complete the project submission form with detailed information
  2. Upload supporting documentation to verify your project
  3. Our team reviews your submission (typically within 2-3 business days)
  4. Once approved, your project appears on our donation platform
  5. Donors can contribute directly to your project

Terms & Conditions

Project Guidelines

To increase your project's chances of approval, please ensure:

  • Your project directly benefits communities affected in Northern or Southern Israel
  • You provide clear, specific details about how funds will be used
  • Your funding request is proportional to the project's scope
  • You include sufficient documentation to verify your project's legitimacy
  • Contact information is valid and regularly monitored

Note: Submitting false information will result in immediate rejection and may lead to permanent exclusion from our platform.
