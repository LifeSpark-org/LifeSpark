FAQs

How does blockchain donation work?

Blockchain donations use cryptocurrency to transfer funds directly and transparently. Each transaction is recorded on a public ledger that anyone can verify, ensuring complete transparency and security.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Donations may be tax-deductible depending on your country's regulations regarding cryptocurrency donations. We recommend consulting with a tax professional for advice specific to your situation.

How can I track my donation?

After making a donation, you'll receive a transaction hash that you can use to track your donation on the blockchain. You can also view impact reports on our platform.
