About Us

Transparency, Trust, and Direct Impact

Our Mission

We are dedicated to helping communities in need through secure and transparent donations. lifeSpark bridges the gap between donors and recipients using blockchain technology to ensure every contribution creates direct impact.

Our vision is to create a world where generosity flows without barriers, where those with resources can directly help those in need with full transparency and minimal overhead.

Our Team

Omer Sabag

Co-Founder & Full-Stack Developer

Avital Gelper

Co-Founder & Full-Stack Developer

lifeSpark was created by Omer Sabag and Avital Gelper, who collaborated equally on every aspect of this platform. Together, they built the entire system from the ground up - implementing the blockchain infrastructure, developing backend systems, and crafting the user interface. Both founders share the same level of expertise across all technical domains of the project, working in tandem to create a transparent donation system that directly connects donors with communities in need. Their unified vision of leveraging technology for humanitarian aid continues to drive the development and improvement of the lifeSpark platform.
